Management Portal

User Managment Portal:

  • Users: Here you can manage users/vendors (Create, View, Delete)
  • Orders: Orders can be edited/deleted or simply viewed
  • Newsletters: Create and send newsletters

Reservation Management

In Store pickups:

  • View Orders: View any pending orders and the details
  • Complete Orders: Mark any pending orders as completed upon pickup
  • Expired Orders: View expired orders and warn customer
